2019 legislative session wrap-up

The 2019 session kicked-off the biennium in underwhelming style, but stayed away of big controversies. Mostly.

By the end of the session, the Democratic overwhelming majority in both chambers of the State House wouldn't to agree on what the priority was, and failed to pass a signature piece of legislation, while Governor Phil Scott stands as the biggest winner of the session.
